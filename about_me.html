Dr Argyroula Kalaitzaki

Dr Argyroula Kalaitzaki (BA, MSc, Ph.D., CPsychol). I am an Associate Professor of Clinical Psychology, Department of Social Work at the Hellenic Mediterranean University (HMU), the head of the Laboratory of Interdisciplinary Approaches for the Enhancement of Quality of Life (Quality of Life Lab; QoL), and the head of the postgraduate program “Interdisciplinary Management of Chronic Diseases, Disability, and Ageing”. I am also an affiliated researcher at the “AgriFood and Life Sciences Institute”, deputy head of the Research Ethics and Ethics Committee, and member of the Training and Lifelong Learning Center at HMU. I was the head of the Department of Social Work at the Hellenic Mediterranean University (HMU) from February 2022- February 2024. I received a ΒΑ in Psychology (1992), an M.Sc. (1996), and a Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology (2000) from the University of Crete, Greece, and a second Ph.D. (2005) from the University of Sunderland, U.K. I have also been appointed the European Diploma in Psychological Therapies (European Institute of Psychotherapy). I have many peer-reviewed papers, chapters in books, and presentations at national and international conferences, co-authored a book in English, and co-edited three books and chapters in books. I have participated in many national and international research projects, with or without funding, and have 22 years of educational experience in higher education. My research interests include, among others, interpersonal relationships, social networks/capital, violence and aggression, parenting/discipline methods, mental disorders and psychotherapy, aging, dementia and Alzheimer’s disease, and recently Positive Psychology (e.g., psychological resilience, positive emotions and relations, posttrauymatic growth).
