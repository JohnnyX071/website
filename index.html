Hello, I’m Dr. Argyroula Kalaitzaki

I am an Associate Professor of Clinical Psychology, Department of Social Work at the Hellenic Mediterranean University (HMU). I am also the director of the Laboratory of Interdisciplinary Approaches for the Enhancement of Quality of Life (Quality of Life Lab; QoL), the director of the postgraduate program "Interdisciplinary Management of Chronic Diseases, Disability, and Ageing" and the president of the Gender Equality Committee of HMU. My scholarly interests, in light of both psychopathology and Positive Psychology, focus on:

  1. Interpersonal relationships
  2. Older people, dementia, and Alzheimer's disease
  3. Traumatic experiences
  4. Health Literacy

About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Clinical Psychology in the Department of Social Work at Hellenic Mediterranean University (HMU). I direct the Laboratory of Interdisciplinary Approaches for the Enhancement of Quality of Life, the postgraduate program "Interdisciplinary Management of Chronic Diseases, Disability, and Ageing," and I am the president of the Gender Equality Committee of HMU.
